Mamaliga

Desctription

A dish that captures the very essence of resourceful, humble, and wholesome Moldovan homecooking, Mamaliga has been one of the go-to staples to feed Moldovan families a hearty meal for generations.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 cup cornmeal(medium grind)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tbsp butter (optional)

Steps

  1. First, in a pot, over high heat, boil the water and add salt.

  2. Once the water boils, turn the heat/flame to medium. And then, pour in the cornmeal slowly while mixing with a wooden spoon.


  3. Keep stirring with the wooden spoon. Add the butter if you’re using any.


  4. Keep stirring until the mamaliga is ready in about 10-12 minutes.


  5. You’ll want to smooth out the mamaliga using the back of a spoon.


  6. Once smoothed out, the mamaliga in the pan should look similar to this.


  7. Turn the fire off. Put the lid on. Leave it to steam for 4-5 minutes.


  8. Then turn the heat high for one minute. You should hear a faint ‘pop,’ which is the bottom of the mamaliga unsticking from the bottom of the pot. Now, you can flip it.
